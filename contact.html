About Us

Fonteyn Holiday Parks is a traditional Dutch family business that has been operating for over 50 years. It all started when Frits Fonteyn opened a small campsite in the south of the country. Today, Fonteyn Holiday Parks operates multiple parks across the Netherlands, as well as several in Belgium and Germany.
